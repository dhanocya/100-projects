Flowing with Hope: Cancer Awareness

Cancer is not a single disease, but a complex group of more than 100 diseases that share a common, life-altering characteristic: the uncontrolled growth and spread of abnormal cells within the body. In a healthy body, cells follow an orderly process of growth, division, and death, guided by a set of genetic instructions. Cancer occurs when specific gene changes disrupt this balance, causing cells to ignore normal "stop" signals and multiply relentlessly.
